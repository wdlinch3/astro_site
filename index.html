Thomas Jefferson High School for Science and Technology

Astronomy Research Lab

The TJHSST Astronomy Research Lab gives students hands-on experience with observational astronomy, astrophysics, and data-driven research. Students explore the universe through telescope imaging, computational analysis, and independent projects on stars, galaxies, exoplanets, and cosmic events.

Research Focus

Student teams investigate variable stars, orbital motion, spectroscopy, and deep-sky imaging.

Tools

The lab combines telescopes, image processing software, Python analysis, and astronomical databases.

Mission

Students learn to ask precise scientific questions, collect evidence, and communicate discoveries clearly.
